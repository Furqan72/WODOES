Definition List


Mango

The mango fruit is roughly oval in shape. The fruit is a drupe, with an outer fresh surrounding a stone. The flesh is soft and bright yeollow-orange in color. The skin of the fruit is yeollo-green to red.

Apple

The apple is a pome(fleshy) fruit in which the ripened ovary and the surrounding tissue both become fleshy and edible. ...when harvested, apples are usually roundish, 5-10 cm (2-4 inches) in diameter, and some of shade of red, green and yellow in color, they vary in size, shape, and acidity depending on the variety.

Grapes

Grapes are fleshy, rounded fruits that grow in clusters made up of many fruits of greenish, yellowish or purple skin. The pulp is jucity and sweet and it contains several seeds or pips. It is a well-known fruit; it is eaten raw, although it is mainly used for making wine.
